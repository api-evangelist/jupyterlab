The Jupyter Book project has content in a bunch of different repositories — user docs , a blog , a team compass , and project & community pages . We recently finished an initiative to serve each set of content at jupyterbook.org, while keeping the source files separated in their repositories. Jupyter Book builds tools and standards for communicating and publishing computational narratives.
